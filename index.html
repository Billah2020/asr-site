About Us

Who we are

Ahmed Sheikh Roy & Co. is a new partnership firm of Chartered Accountants. Its journey started on 01 October 2019 to provide world class services and solutions to its clients. We caringly call us ASR. The Firm is poised to assist clients of all sizes and nature, from SMEs to mid-caps and global conglomerates as well as start-ups, NGOs and public bodies at every stage of their developments.

The team is an excellent combination of public accountant and seasoned corporate professionals. Two of the partners have 20 years of experiences in the corporate landscape of the country, and the other partner has excellent combination of both as a public accountant and a corporate professional. They are highly educated, extensively trained and professionally groomed Chartered Accountants.

The partners, during their careers with corporates and as public accountant, have consistently delivered globally recognized exceptional results over the last 20 years across the industries. They have led, worked, and resolved challenges in multinational complex manufacturing and supply chain contexts and local big conglomerates. Their collective experiences span across FMCG, consumer durables, heavy industry, Companies listed in stock exchanges, international and local development organisations, UN agencies, service sector, government bodies and banks.

Apart from audit and assurances and tax matters, the Firm specializes in business process reengineering, digital transformation, enterprise risk management, financial and operational performance optimization, supply chain management, legal services, human capital management, and economic and business research.
