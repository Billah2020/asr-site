Our Leader

Anjan Kumer Roy FCA

Anjan Kumer Roy, FCA is Partner at Ahmed Sheikh Roy & Co. He qualified as a chartered accountant from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Bangladesh (ICAB) and holds an MBA and BBA in Accounting & Information Systems from Faculty of Business Studies (FBS), University of Dhaka with distinction of “Dean Honours List”. He has also obtained Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in Information Technology from IIT, Jahangirnagar University and obtained training on Advance Business Valuation Techniques from Business Valuation International, London, UK.

Anjan has worked as a Partner of Mak & Co., Chartered Accountants that representing SFAI, USA and also worked as a founder partner of ARUN & ANJAN, Chartered Accountants. Anjan worked as Manager – Audit & Consultancy of SF Ahmed & Co., Chartered Accountants that represented Ernst & Young (E&Y) in Bangladesh and served multinational and local listed and non-listed bank, non-banking financial institution, manufacturing companies and NGOs.

Anjan has also worked as part-time faculty member of Bangladesh Institute of Capital Market (BICM), different colleges under National University. He teaches accounting, audit and financial management. He also served ICAB as an active member in different standing committees.
